The Outcast tells the story of the "Outcasts" - a group of superpower bearers dwelling in the city. While they look no different from any other human, they each bear remarkable skills of their own. In Season 4, we delve into the arc of Biyou Village: "Temp-worker" Chen Duo had murdered the South China region person-in-charge and fled southwest. Temp-workers from the other areas received orders to arrest her; only to run into Ma Hongxian, who hails from Biyou Village...
